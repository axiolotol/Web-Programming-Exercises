Chef Meowsey's Purrfect Beef Wellington

A gourmet dish perfected by the Cat Gordon Ramsay

Ingredients

  • Beef fillet (about 1kg)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Olive oil
  • 2 tbsp English mustard
  • 500g mushrooms (finely chopped)
  • 4 slices of prosciutto
  • 500g puff pastry
  • 1 egg yolk (beaten, for egg wash)
  • Flour (for dusting)

Method

  1. Season the beef fillet with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sear the beef on all sides.
  3. Brush the beef with mustard and let it cool.
  4. Sauté mushrooms until soft, then let them cool.
  5. Lay prosciutto slices on a cling film sheet and spread the mushrooms on top.
  6. Place the beef on top and wrap tightly, then refrigerate.
  7. Roll out the puff pastry and place the wrapped beef in the center.
  8. Wrap the pastry around the beef and seal the edges.
  9. Brush with egg yolk and refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  10. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for about 35 minutes.
  11. Rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
