About Arcane

Arcane is an animated steampunk action-adventure series created by Christian Linke and Alex Yee for Netflix. Set in Riot Games' League of Legends universe, the series tells the origin story of two iconic League champions—sisters Vi and Jinx—and their complex relationship amidst the escalating unrest between the utopian city of Piltover and its oppressed undercity, Zaun.

With its groundbreaking animation style, compelling narrative, and deep character development, Arcane has received universal acclaim from critics and viewers alike, winning multiple Emmy Awards and setting a new standard for video game adaptations.
