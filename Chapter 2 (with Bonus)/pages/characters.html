Main Characters

Violet (Vi)

A tough, no-nonsense fighter from the undercity of Zaun. Vi struggles with trauma from her childhood and a fierce desire to protect her sister Powder. After years of imprisonment, she allies with Caitlyn Kiramman to investigate crimes in the undercity while searching for her long-lost sister.

Jinx (formerly Powder)

Once a sweet, eager-to-please child named Powder, Jinx transformed into an unpredictable and dangerous criminal following childhood trauma. Her abandonment issues and mental instability, fostered by Silco's influence, manifest in her chaotic behavior and technological genius.

Jayce Talis

A brilliant inventor who pioneers Hextech technology in Piltover. Jayce's journey takes him from idealistic scientist to pragmatic politician as he navigates the consequences of his creations and the complex politics of Piltover's Council.

Viktor

A scientist from Zaun who ascended to Piltover's Academy through sheer brilliance. Viktor suffers from a degenerative condition, driving his research into human evolution and augmentation. His desire to help humanity often conflicts with ethical boundaries.

Caitlyn Kiramman

Born to a wealthy Piltover family, Caitlyn rejects her privileged upbringing to become an Enforcer. Determined and principled, she sees beyond the prejudices separating Piltover from Zaun. Her partnership with Vi begins as a necessity but evolves into something deeper.

Silco

The ruthless crime lord of Zaun who raises Jinx after Vi's disappearance. Despite his criminal enterprises and willingness to use violence, Silco genuinely loves Jinx as a daughter and dreams of an independent nation of Zaun.

Ekko

A brilliant young inventor from the undercity who leads a group called the Firelights. Once childhood friends with Powder, Ekko now stands against Jinx and what she represents. His ingenuity led to the creation of time-manipulation technology.

Heimerdinger

Heimerdinger is an ancient yordle scientist who represents the intellectual power of Piltover. He plays a significant role in the political landscape, advocating for progress and innovation while cautioning against the uncontrolled use of powerful technologies like Hextech.

Mel Medarda

Mel is a key figure in Piltover's political and business world. She is pragmatic, calculating, and highly influential. Throughout the series, her alliance with Jayce deepens as they work together to steer Piltover’s future while navigating the complexities of power and corruption.

Vander

Vander is the moral backbone of the undercity, serving as a father figure to Vi and Powder. A former enforcer turned peaceful protector, he dreams of a better future for Zaun and strives to maintain order in the chaos of the undercity.

Supporting Characters

Mylo

One of Vander's adopted children and Vi's close friend. Mylo is skeptical and often mocks Powder, but his loyalty to the group is unwavering. His tragic fate plays a crucial role in shaping Jinx's transformation.

Claggor

Another of Vander's adopted children, Claggor is strong, dependable, and always looks out for his friends. His loss is a significant turning point in the story, deeply impacting Vi and Powder.

Sevika

Sevika is a loyal enforcer of Silco, serving as a merciless bodyguard and enforcer. She is tough, pragmatic, and is determined to bring order to the lawless undercity.

Ambessa

Ambessa is a powerful, manipulative figure in Piltover's elite class. She plays a critical role in the political and business world, often using her influence to further her own interests.

Singed (Dr. Corin Reveck)

Dr. Corin Reveck, also known as Singed, is a brilliant yet twisted scientist. He is responsible for experimenting with dangerous chemicals and mutagenic substances, creating the Shimmer drug, and having a direct hand in the events that lead to Jinx’s transformation.

Marcus

Marcus is a conflicted Enforcer who struggles with his moral compass. As a lawman caught between his duty and the realities of life in Zaun, he often finds himself at odds with his own values.
