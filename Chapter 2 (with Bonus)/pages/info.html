Key Information

Production Details

  • Created by: Christian Linke and Alex Yee
  • Production: Fortiche Production, Riot Games
  • Distributed by: Netflix
  • First Season: Premiered on November 6, 2021 (9 episodes)
  • Second Season: Premiered on November 23, 2024 (9 episodes)

Technical Specifications

  • Animation Style: Combination of 2D and 3D animation with hand-painted textures
  • Runtime: Approximately 40 minutes per episode
  • Languages: Available in multiple languages with dubbing and subtitles
  • Rating: TV-14 (US), 16+ (most regions)

Awards and Recognition

  • Emmy Awards: 4 wins including Outstanding Animated Program (2022)
  • Annie Awards: 9 wins including Best TV/Media - Limited Series
  • Rotten Tomatoes: 100% critic score, 96% audience score
  • IMDb Rating: 9.0/10

Music

  • Composer: Alex Seaver of Mako
  • Opening Theme: "Enemy" by Imagine Dragons ft. JID
  • Closing Theme: "Goodbye" by Ramsey
  • Notable Songs: "Playground" by Bea Miller, "Guns for Hire" by Woodkid
